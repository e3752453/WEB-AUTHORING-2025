Passion for Web Development

My name is Plamedie, and I am passionate about web development. Currently, I'm in my second year of college, pursuing a degree in web development. My Journey as an International Student

I was born and raised in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (RDC), and my journey as an international student has been both challenging and rewarding. Moving abroad for my education has pushed me out of my comfort zone, teaching me resilience and adaptability. Adjusting to a new environment while keeping up with academic demands hasn’t always been easy, but I embrace every opportunity to grow. My background has shaped my perspective and given me the determination to work hard and succeed in my chosen field.

The Importance of Family

Family is one of the most important aspects of my life. No matter where I am, I always make an effort to stay connected with my loved ones. Being far from home has made me appreciate even more the bonds that hold us together. I value deep, meaningful relationships, and I try to bring warmth and sincerity into my friendships and collaborations. Having a strong support system gives me motivation and keeps me grounded.
